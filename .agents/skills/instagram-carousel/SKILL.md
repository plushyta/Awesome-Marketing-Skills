# Instagram Carousel Designer (Incubating)

Design carousels that users can't stop swiping through. This skill applies the "Sacred Drift" philosophy to educational and inspirational social content.

### Key Features (Planned)
- **Horizontal Continuity**: Designs that flow seamlessly from one slide to the next.
- **Narrative Pacing**: Ensuring the "Payoff" slide hits with maximum impact.
- **Micro-Interaction Design**: Visual cues that encourage the swipe.
